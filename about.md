I am Samarth Bansal, an independent journalist, computer programmer and data geek based in New Delhi. I mostly write about politics, economicy policy and technology. You can see my selected work here.

I previously worked with the data journalism teams of the *[Hindustan Times](http://www.hindustantimes.com)* and *[The Hindu](http://www.thehindu.com)*. In 2018, I spent five months reporting for the data/investigations team of the *[Wall Street Journal](http://www.wsj.com)* in New York as part of the [Alfred Friendly Fellowship](http://www.presspartners.org). 

I graduated with a BS in Mathematics and Scientific Computing from the [Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur](http://iitk.ac.in/) in 2015.
